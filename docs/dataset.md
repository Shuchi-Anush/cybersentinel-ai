The CICIDS2017 dataset is structured to provide comprehensive information about various network traffic patterns, including both normal and malicious activities. The dataset includes a wide range of features that capture different aspects of network traffic, such as flow duration, packet size, and protocol type.
Each entry in the dataset is labeled to indicate whether it represents normal traffic or a specific type of attack. The labels are crucial for training machine learning models to detect and classify different types of cyber threats effectively.
